About Me page

Biography

I have spent most of my professional journey in the financial sector, starting as a banker before moving into my current role as an Auditor at the Central Bank of Malaysia. My work focuses on governance, compliance, and risk assessment ensuring that work processes & procedures run with precision and integrity. Alongside this, I continue to grow as a student, constantly learning and adapting to new frameworks, regulations, and technology.

Outside of the office, music plays an important part in my life. As a guitarist and Malay traditional band member of ARMADA DENDANG MALAYSIA, I balance structure and discipline with creativity and culture. This blend of numbers and notes shapes how I see the world: analytical yet expressive, formal yet deeply human.

Personal Interests

  • Exploring traditional Malay music and cultural heritage through live performances.
  • Studying in SARJANA MUDA SAINS MAKLUMAT (KEPUJIAN) PENGURUSAN SISTEM MAKLUMAT at UiTM Shah Alam.
  • Recording and arranging guitar pieces that mix modern and traditional elements.
  • Attending and experiencing a spiritual suluk at PULAU MANIS, TERENGGANU.
